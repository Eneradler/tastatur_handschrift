About TRIS Project

This project was developed as a university course task.


Typing rythm or typing dynamics refers to the automated method of identifying or confirming the identity of an individual based on the manner and the rhythm of typing on a keyboard. Typing dynamics is a behavioral biometric, this means that the biometric factor is ‘something you do’.
